Solar Cells:
Solar Cells are based off of perovskite crystal structures which is being used as the greater alternative with the efficiency being able to go up to 40%, though brings up challenges including the lack of long term stability and can become more toxic overtime. Solar Cells help solar panels use the energy they collect from the sunlight and transferring it into energy that we can use on a daily basis as an alternative for fossil fuels. for Example, using Hydrogen and Ammonia as replacements for Fossil Fuels. Solar Cells are made through natural gas and allows Co2 to be captured directly, which is important because this process collecting Co2 can get expensive.
