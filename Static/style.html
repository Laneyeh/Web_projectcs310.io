Welcome to my website!

So.. What is this Website for???

Code Green is an informative website about the benefits of computer scientists towards climate and environmental issues while also containing fun designs, inforgraphis, and easy to digest information that everyone can understand.

My Hopes for the Website:

I hope that making an informative website about this important issue that it would not only make computer scientists feel more secure in their career choice and their impact on the environment and the world around them. I hope that this will also encourage the study of Computer Science and its so important towards the impact of the Earth's future environment.

About the Creator:

Hello! My name is Laney Humble and I am a sophomore at Elizabethtown College for a dual major in Computer Science and Graphic Design. This website is mainly made as a project for my Web Development class, but a topic that I find important when it comes to the impact of computer science on the world.
