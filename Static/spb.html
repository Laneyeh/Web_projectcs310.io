With every passing moment, solar panels are becoming more efficient and less exprensive every year, but how can we derive even more energy from a solar plant working as computer scientists? One solution that has been in the works since 1978, is a solar computer. Solar Panels have become capable of charging computers directly, which helps when charging computers directly which help charge electronics. Some mechanic magazines in 1978 thought of a do-it-yourself solar energy commputer to combat this issue.The end product would charge the battery during the day with the battery powering the computer during the night. Though this system could be functional, it was not as practical or efficient to be used on a daily basis. But these magazines started the ideas around the Solar Computer.

While solar computers don't exist yet, there are two main methods to run a computer based on solar power

  1. Using a home solar power system to charge your electronic devices
  2. Deriving energy completely from a solar plant
Through whatever way you make a solar computer, they will need to rely heavily on solar panels as they are BIG converters for energy from the sunlight and turning it into electrical energy. Through using solar computers, you will save money on your electricity bills, you PC will always have power, and you are using energy to power your computer rather than fossil fuels.
